# Audio credits

Prototype audio is streamed from the public repository
`lavenderdotpet/CC0-Public-Domain-Sounds`, licensed CC0 1.0.

Selected packs:
- `40-cc0-water-splash-slime-sfx`: water, rain and splash recordings.
- `30-cc0-sfx-loops`: machine/engine and pump loops.
- `50-cc0-sci-fi-sfx`: sonar and interface cues.
- `100-CC0-wood-metal-SFX` and `100-cc0-sfx-2`: rope, collision and repair contact sounds.
